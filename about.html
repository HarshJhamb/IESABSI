Who We Are

Truly a One-Stop
Logistics Solution

AB STOP India is a team of professionals specialising in exhibition logistics. We provide quality and full-range cargo service for shows in India and overseas.

AB STOP India is a truly One-Stop logistics solution for exhibitions, conferences, concerts, theatre, film, TV, sports events or any other time critical consignments. Whether you are an international exhibitor attending a trade show at Bharat Mandapam or sending a film crew's equipment to Mumbai — we have the expertise, the network, and the presence to make it happen.

"Once you try our service you are sure to become our regular customer."

We are grateful to exhibitors, stand builders, and associate partners worldwide for their trust and continued support. Our partnerships span customs authorities, official freight forwarders, venue operators, and on-site handling crews across all major Indian exhibition centres.

Our Specialities

We Are Always
At Your Back of Hand

Especially when you need expert support with any of these critical logistics requirements:

Temporary Exportation / ATA Carnet

For exhibits shipped overseas from India to an international show and returning back.

Temporary Importation for India Shows

Bringing international exhibits to India under ATA Carnet or temporary import bond with full re-export compliance.

Final Import of Giveaways & Souvenirs

Catalogues, brochures, samples and souvenirs imported permanently for distribution at your India stand.

On-Site Cargo Service

Labour, forklifts, cranes, handling of empties, stand delivery and unpacking — all at the venue floor.

3 Reasons to Feel
Safe & Calm With Us

Every international exhibitor coming to India faces complexity. We remove that complexity entirely.

01

Customs, Deadlines & Documents Are Clear

We manage every document, deadline, and customs procedure. From ATA Carnet applications to temporary import bonds and re-export confirmations — nothing falls through the cracks. You know exactly what to expect and when.

02

All Cargo Issues On-Site Are Solved

Our on-site team is physically at the venue with forklifts, labour, and authority. If something unexpected happens at the exhibition hall — a damaged crate, a delayed truck, a last-minute stand change — we fix it before you even know there was a problem.

03

Your Exhibits Are Delivered In Time

Exhibition deadlines are absolute. There is no "almost on time" at a trade show. Our track record of zero delays across hundreds of international shipments speaks to our commitment to your schedule — always, without exception.
