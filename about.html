AB STOP India is a team of professionals specialising in exhibition logistics across India and overseas.

We provide quality and full-range cargo service for shows in India and around the world. AB STOP India is a truly One-Stop logistics solution for exhibitions, conferences, concerts, theatre, film, TV, sports events, and any other time-critical consignments.

Whether you are an international exhibitor attending a trade show at Bharat Mandapam or sending a film crew's equipment to Mumbai — we have the expertise, the network, and the presence to make it happen.

Our Specialities

Always At Your
Back of Hand

Especially when you need expert support with any of these critical logistics requirements:

Temporary Exportation / ATA Carnet

For exhibits shipped overseas from India to an international show and returning back. Full documentation managed end-to-end.

Temporary Importation for India Shows

Bringing international exhibits to India under ATA Carnet or temporary import bond with full re-export compliance.
