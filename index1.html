Frequently Asked Questions

Everything international exhibitors need to know about bringing cargo to India for exhibitions.

Can I use an ATA Carnet for my exhibition cargo to India?

Yes. India is a member of the ATA Carnet system. AB STOP India has extensive experience processing ATA Carnets for exhibition goods at all Indian customs stations. We manage all endorsements, customs liaison, and ensure the Carnet is properly closed after re-export.

How far in advance should I contact AB STOP India?

We recommend contacting us at least 4–6 weeks before your intended shipment date for air freight, and 8–10 weeks for sea freight. This allows sufficient time for documentation preparation, customs registration, and venue coordination. However, we also handle urgent and last-minute shipments.

What documents are required for exhibition cargo to India?

Standard requirements include: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Air Waybill / Bill of Lading, Exhibition Organiser participation confirmation, and a covering letter on company letterhead. For ATA Carnet shipments, the Carnet document replaces the commercial invoice. Additional documents may be required for regulated goods (food, medical devices, chemicals, etc.).

Is temporary import available for exhibition goods in India?

Yes. Exhibition goods can be imported into India on a temporary basis under a customs bond, with a guarantee that the goods will be re-exported after the exhibition. This avoids payment of import duties. AB STOP India manages all temporary import formalities and bond administration on your behalf.

Can I ship food and beverage samples for exhibition in India?

Yes, but food and beverage products require additional clearances from FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority of India) and phytosanitary clearances for certain products. Our team will guide you through the specific requirements for your product category, and manage all liaison with regulatory authorities.

What happens to my empty cases during the exhibition?

AB STOP India collects all empty cases (crates, boxes, packaging) from your stand after unpacking and stores them in our dedicated empty case storage facility at or near the venue. At the end of the show, we return them to your stand for repacking before the move-out.

Can you handle very heavy or oversized machinery exhibits?

Yes. We regularly handle heavy plant, industrial machinery, and oversized exhibits including vehicles for Auto Expo, aircraft components for Aero India, and heavy equipment for IMTEX. We arrange special transport, cranes, and heavy-lift capabilities as required.

Do you provide services across all Indian cities and venues?

Yes. AB STOP India has permanent operations in New Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Greater Noida. We also cover Ahmedabad, Chandigarh, Kolkata, Pune, Jaipur, and other cities where major Indian trade events are held.
