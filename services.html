Service 01

Customs Clearance
& ATA Carnet

Navigating Indian customs for exhibition cargo requires specialist knowledge. We handle every regime, every document, every deadline.

AB STOP India offers the following options of customs clearance for your India exhibition shipments. All shipments to AB STOP India confirm that you totally accept and agree with our conditions of service.

Temporary Importation for Exhibits — Full ATA Carnet management with guaranteed re-export after the show. We monitor deadlines so you never face a customs penalty.
Final Importation for Giveaways — Catalogues, brochures, samples, and souvenirs imported permanently with correct HS codes and duty management.
Bond & Bank Guarantee Customs — Where ATA Carnet is not applicable, we manage cash bond or bank guarantee procedures with Indian customs authorities.
Re-Export Documentation — Complete paperwork for goods leaving India after the show, ensuring zero customs complications.

Important Information

Must Know

Essential information about our on-site handling operations.

01

All goods must be in appropriate packing. AB STOP India is not responsible for damage caused to goods inside packing that has no external signs of damage.

02

All risks of our operations are insured. However, handling of exhibits weighing 3 tonnes or more requires preliminary technical consultation with AB STOP India.

03

Each forklift truck is equipped with 2-metre long forks with special holes & hooks — allowing lifting of cargo that cannot be taken with usual forks (ropes only).

04

Average capacity of each forklift truck is 3 tonnes, with an average maximum lift height of 2 metres. Forklift operation is permitted for AB STOP India staff only.

05

Payment invoice will be issued within one working day of receiving your order form. Please ensure payment before the operation begins on-site.

06

All temporary import permits and ATA Carnets must reach our India office a minimum of 5 working days before the exhibition opening date.

07

Re-export documentation must be filed within the validity period of the ATA Carnet or temporary import bond. Late re-export incurs customs penalties and we cannot be responsible for delays caused by exhibitor error.

08

Emergency 24/7 support is available for time-critical shipment issues. Contact our duty manager at any hour for exhibition emergencies.
