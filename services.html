Customs & Compliance

Customs &
ATA Carnet

India's customs procedures are among the most complex for international exhibitors. Our specialists manage every regime, document, and deadline — compliantly and on time.

AB STOP India offers complete customs handling for your India exhibition shipments. We are ATA Carnet specialists, managing the entire temporary importation lifecycle from entry to re-export.

ATA Carnet Management

Full end-to-end ATA Carnet handling — endorsement on arrival, monitoring of validity, and guaranteed re-export before expiry.

Final Importation for Giveaways

Correct HS code classification and duty management for catalogues, brochures, samples, and promotional items retained in India.

Bond & Bank Guarantee Customs

Where ATA Carnet is inapplicable, we manage cash bond or bank guarantee procedures with Indian customs authorities directly.

Complete Re-Export Documentation

All paperwork for goods leaving India after the show, ensuring zero complications at the exit port.

Deadline Management

We track all customs validity periods and proactively alert you to critical deadlines before they become penalties.

ATA Carnet Temporary Import Duty Management Re-Export
All temporary import permits and ATA Carnets must reach our India office a minimum of 5 working days before the exhibition opens.

Before You Book

Essential Things to Know

Key operational details about our on-site handling and customs procedures.

01
Packaging Standards

All goods must arrive in appropriate export packaging. We are not liable for internal damage where there are no external signs of damage to the casing.

02
Heavy Exhibit Protocol

All operations are insured. However, exhibits weighing 3 tonnes or more require preliminary technical consultation before we can confirm handling.

03
Forklift Specification

Each forklift has 2-metre forks with special attachment hooks — allowing lifting of cargo that cannot be lifted with standard forks or ropes alone.

04
Capacity & Height

Average capacity is 3 tonnes per lift with max lift height of 2 metres. Forklift operation is exclusively performed by AB STOP India trained staff.

05
Payment Schedule

Invoice issued within one working day of receiving your order form. Payment must be confirmed before on-site operations commence.

06
ATA Carnet Deadline

All temporary import permits and ATA Carnets must reach our India office a minimum of 5 working days before the exhibition opening date.

07
Re-Export Responsibility

Re-export documentation must be filed within the ATA Carnet validity period. Penalties caused by exhibitor delays are not our responsibility.

08
24/7 Emergency Line

Emergency support is available around the clock for time-critical shipment issues. Contact our duty manager directly at any hour for exhibition emergencies.
